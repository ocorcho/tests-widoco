Abstract

This is the human and machine readable Vocabulary/Ontology governed by the European Union Agency for Railways (https://www.era.europa.eu/). It represents the concepts and relationships linked to the sectorial legal framework and the use cases under the Agency´s remit, as described in the Commission Implementing Regulation (EU) [to be updated after publication] on the common specifications for the register of railway infrastructure [to be updated after publication].

This documentation presents the European Vehicle Register (EVR) concepts have been included in the Vocabulary according to the document of the COMMISSION IMPLEMENTING DECISION (EU) 2018/1614 of 25 October 2018 (https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/dec_impl/2018/1614).
